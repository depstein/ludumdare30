Solar Annihilation

Objective:

Stay alive as long as possible. Corral sheep into their 'pens' (the brown patches on the ground) to regain health. Cast spells on archers and rabbits that are getting in your way, but be careful! Doing so will increase your 'violence level', causing archers to spawn more quickly.

Controls:

WASD or Arrow Keys: movement
Space Bar: 'shout'-any nearby sheep will run away from you
Left mouse button: cast a spell where your mouse is
Holding right mouse button: draw a gesture to curve your spell, then press the left mouse button to cast

The 'Vision':

You are struggling to keep their sanity in a post-apocalyptic world. As a result, you 'pretend' that your environment is much more normal than it appears. You're just corralling sheep into their pens! Shooting rabbits that are trying to get berries! However, as time elapses, you lose sanity and the hopelessness of your situation becomes more and more apparent, until finally, you can't take it anymore.

... obviously this vision was not fully realized, and in the interest of creating something playable, 'Sheep Shot' exists in the form you see here. Feel free to ask me about our experiences, specifically around 'onioning' and how we'll do better next time.
